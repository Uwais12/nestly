Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2024

At Nestly, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address to create and secure your account.

Content: We store the links (URLs) you save to Nestly, along with any notes or tags you add.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics (like app crashes or feature usage) to improve the app stability.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the core service: saving and organizing your links.
  • To generate metadata: We fetch public information (like title, image, and description) for the links you save to display them nicely in your feed.
  • To improve the app: We use anonymous analytics to fix bugs and understand which features are most useful.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database provider. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API to automatically categorize your saved links. Only the text content (title, description) of the link is sent for processing; no personal user data is shared with OpenAI for this purpose.

5. Your Rights

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at support.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@nestly.app
